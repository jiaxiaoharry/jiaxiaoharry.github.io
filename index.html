Biography [CV]

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electronic Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Max Q.-H. Meng. Before that, I received the B. Eng. degree in the Department of Control Science and Engineering from Shandong University in 2014. I worked with Prof. Wei Zhang for undergraduate research.

My research focuses on the development of novel AI strategies for medical image analysis. Recently, I am working on deep learning-driven neural networks for lesion diagnosis in endoscopic images.
